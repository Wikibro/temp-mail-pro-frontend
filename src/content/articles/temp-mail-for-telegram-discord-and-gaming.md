## Why Gamers and Community Users Need Separation

Gaming and chat platforms can generate frequent notifications, security prompts, and promotional emails. Temporary inboxes help keep those messages isolated.

## Best Use Cases

- testing new communities and servers
- creating separate identities for experiments
- avoiding exposure of personal email on new platforms

## Good Account Hygiene

1. Use one inbox per platform.
2. Save verification and recovery details early.
3. Do not mass-create accounts too quickly.

## Bottom Line

Temp mail is useful for testing and privacy on gaming and chat platforms, especially when you keep inboxes separated by platform.
