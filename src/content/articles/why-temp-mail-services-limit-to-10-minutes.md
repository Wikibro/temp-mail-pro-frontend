## The Main Reason: Cost and Abuse Control

Running disposable email infrastructure costs money. Short windows reduce storage needs and help limit abuse patterns.

## Other Operational Reasons

- reduced server load
- easier inbox cleanup
- lower moderation burden
- simpler anti-spam enforcement

## User Impact

Short limits work for instant OTP tasks but fail for delayed confirmations, trial reminders, and staged verification flows.

## Better Alternative

Services that offer configurable duration provide more flexibility while still protecting user privacy.

## Bottom Line

The 10-minute model exists for operational efficiency, not because it is always best for user workflows.
