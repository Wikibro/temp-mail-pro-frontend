## Privacy Risks on Craigslist

Craigslist is one of the largest classifieds platforms—and one of the most at-risk for email scraping and spam. Common exposures include:

- **Harvesting bots** that scan listings for contact info
- **Spam campaigns** targeting Craigslist users
- **Scam coordination** through email chains
- **Identity exposure** if posting items or services

Temporary email isolates your main inbox from these risks.

## Does Craigslist Accept Temporary Email?

Yes, Craigslist accepts temporary email for account creation and replies. You can:

- Create a Craigslist account with a disposable address
- Reply to listings using a temporary inbox
- Post your own items without exposing your real email

Craigslist does not have strict domain filtering, so temporary email works reliably.

## Using Temp Email on Craigslist Safely

### For Account Creation

1. Create a temporary email address
2. Sign up for Craigslist with that address
3. Verify your email address
4. Use the account for buying, selling, or browsing

### For Replies to Listings

If you are interested in a Craigslist item but want to offer a reply email, use a temporary address instead of your personal one. This keeps your primary inbox safe from spam if the seller or listing turns out to be problematic.

### For Posting Items

When selling items on Craigslist:

1. Create a Craigslist account with temporary email
2. Post your item listing
3. Direct buyers to reply through Craigslist messages
4. The account expires after a set period; create a new one for future listings

## Security Tips for Craigslist

**Don't:**
- Share your temporary email in the listing (buyers should reply through Craigslist)
- Use the same temporary email for multiple high-value listings
- Keep sensitive details in email replies

**Do:**
- Use Craigslist's built-in messaging when possible
- Meet in safe public locations
- Verify buyer or seller credibility before exchanging personal info
- Store order details or transaction confirmations separately from email

## When Temp Email Is Best on Craigslist

Temporary email shines when you:

- Post one-time items you are selling
- Browse and reply to occasional listings
- Want to test the market without spam overload
- Are concerned about data mining

## When Not to Use Temp Email on Craigslist

Avoid temporary email for:

- Long-term seller accounts (you may lose access to customer inquiries)
- High-value transactions (you need reliable account recovery)
- Community engagement (building reputation requires consistent identity)

For accounts where you plan to sell multiple items over time, an alias or custom domain is more reliable. See [Temp Email vs Email Alias: Which Protects Privacy Better?](/blog/temp-email-vs-email-alias) for choosing between them.

## Bottom Line

Temporary email is an effective privacy layer on Craigslist for one-time buyers and sellers. It protects your main inbox from harvesting, spam, and scams while keeping transactions safe and anonymous.
