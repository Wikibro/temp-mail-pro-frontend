## Is It Legal?

In most regions, using temporary email is legal for privacy and spam reduction. Legality depends on intent and behavior.

## What Can Become Illegal

- fraud or identity deception
- bypassing lawful restrictions
- abusing terms of service at scale

## Responsible Use Guidelines

1. Use temp mail for privacy, not deception.
2. Respect platform policies and local laws.
3. Avoid using disposable addresses for regulated services.

## Bottom Line

Temporary email itself is usually legal. Misuse is the risk, not the tool.
