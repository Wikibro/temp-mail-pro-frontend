## The Problem With One Inbox

Using one temporary inbox for every site causes message collisions and slower verification.

## Multi-Email Organization Framework

1. Create one inbox category per intent: trials, shopping, social, testing.
2. Use custom names to identify source quickly.
3. Keep a short note of which inbox belongs to which platform.
4. Retire inboxes once the task ends.

## Benefits

- faster OTP retrieval
- cleaner audit trail for signups
- reduced risk of mixing unrelated accounts

## Bottom Line

Multiple temporary emails are easy to manage when you use clear naming and category-based separation.
