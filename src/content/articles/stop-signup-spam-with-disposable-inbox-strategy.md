## Why Signup Spam Never Ends

Once your primary email enters multiple marketing lists, promotional volume compounds over time.

## A Simple Disposable Strategy

1. Reserve personal email for trusted long-term services.
2. Use temporary inboxes for coupons, trials, and one-time downloads.
3. Segment inboxes by intent so cleanup stays easy.

## Operational Tips

- use custom names for traceability
- rotate inboxes regularly
- keep important activation links in notes

## Bottom Line

A disposable inbox strategy prevents most signup spam before it starts.
