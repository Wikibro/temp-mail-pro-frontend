## Free Trials and Inbox Overload

Many trial funnels trigger follow-up campaigns, upsells, and reminder sequences. Temporary email helps isolate that traffic.

## What to Look For

- fast OTP delivery
- enough expiry for delayed reminders
- simple inbox switching
- custom names for better organization

## Trial Workflow

1. Create a dedicated inbox per trial.
2. Verify the account and save the first activation mail.
3. Monitor follow-up messages during evaluation.
4. If you keep the product, switch to a permanent address.

## Avoid These Mistakes

- using one inbox for every trial
- relying on short-expiry inboxes for long evaluations
- forgetting to migrate retained accounts

## Bottom Line

The best temp email for trials is the one that balances speed, privacy, and enough inbox duration for your real evaluation window.
