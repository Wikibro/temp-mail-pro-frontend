## Why Shopping Signups Create Spam

Store newsletters, abandoned cart reminders, and campaign blasts can quickly flood a personal inbox.

## Better Shopping Setup

Use separate temporary inboxes for:

- coupons and first-order discounts
- marketplace one-time purchases
- seasonal campaign signups

## Benefits

- less marketing clutter in personal email
- cleaner tracking of promo messages
- easier unsubscribe management

## Safety Rules

Do not use disposable inboxes for warranty-critical accounts that you plan to keep for years. Move those to a permanent address.

## Bottom Line

Temporary email is excellent for shopping experiments and promo funnels where privacy and spam control are top priorities.
