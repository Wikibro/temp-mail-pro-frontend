## Core Difference

A temporary email is disposable and short-to-medium term. An alias usually forwards to a permanent mailbox you control.

## Temp Email Wins When

- you want low commitment
- you need fast signups
- you want to avoid exposing your main mailbox

## Alias Wins When

- account recovery is important
- you need long-term continuity
- you want central inbox control with identity separation

## Hybrid Strategy

Use temporary email during evaluation. Switch to alias if the account becomes permanent.

## Bottom Line

Both are useful. Choose based on account importance and recovery needs.
