## Good Use Cases

Temporary email can help with low-priority boards, one-time downloads, and testing unfamiliar platforms.

## Bad Use Cases

Do not use disposable inboxes for serious applications where interview communication and recovery continuity are critical.

## Safer Approach

1. Use temporary email for exploration.
2. Use professional email for target employers.
3. Keep records of where each address was used.

## Bottom Line

Use temp email for discovery and filtering, then switch to a permanent address for important opportunities.
