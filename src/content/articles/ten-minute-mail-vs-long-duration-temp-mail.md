## Quick Comparison

Short-expiry tools are optimized for instant verification. Long-duration temp mail is better for tasks that continue beyond one session.

## Choose 10-Minute Mail If

- you only need one OTP now
- no follow-up emails are expected
- the account is not important later

## Choose Long-Duration Temp Mail If

- trials send reminders over days
- you are testing multiple flows
- you need to revisit messages later
- you manage several temporary identities

## Performance and Reliability

Longer expiry reduces the risk of losing delayed confirmation emails. That is often the main reason users switch from short-expiry tools.

## Bottom Line

Neither model is universally best. Match inbox duration to your real timeline. For repeated workflows, long-duration options are usually more practical.
