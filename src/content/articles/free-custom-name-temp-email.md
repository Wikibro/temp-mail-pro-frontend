## Why Custom Names Are Useful

Random addresses are fine for one-time tasks, but custom names are better when you want control. A custom local-part makes it easier to remember where an address was used and to separate traffic by purpose.

## Benefits of Free Custom-Name Temp Email

- clearer account labeling
- easier inbox filtering during tests
- better tracking for coupon and trial signups
- less confusion when handling multiple disposable addresses

## Practical Naming Strategy

Use names based on destination and purpose, such as `shop-spring`, `trial-design`, or `qa-flow-a`. This makes follow-up messages easier to identify.

## Security Notes

Do not use temporary email for sensitive financial or legal accounts. For long-term services, move to a stable mailbox once the account is permanent.

## Bottom Line

Free custom-name temp email gives you better control than random throwaway addresses. It is one of the simplest improvements for privacy-first signup workflows.
