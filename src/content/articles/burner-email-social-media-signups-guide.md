## Why Social Signups Need Extra Privacy

Social apps often use your email for notifications, growth prompts, and remarketing. A burner address limits exposure.

## Smart Setup

- create a dedicated inbox per platform
- avoid using the same burner for every network
- capture verification and security alerts early

## When to Switch to Permanent Email

If an account becomes business-critical or identity-critical, migrate to a permanent address for reliable recovery.

## Common Errors

- using burner email for high-value creator accounts
- ignoring account security settings after signup

## Bottom Line

Burner email works well for experimentation and privacy-conscious onboarding, but serious long-term accounts should use stable recovery options.
